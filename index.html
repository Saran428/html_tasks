ABOUT US

Techfest at the ABC Institute of Technology Namakkal. This annual festival began in 1998 to provide a platform for Indian students to showcase their projects. It's now Asia's largest science and technology festival, attracting people from all over the world.

Social initiatives: Techfest has social initiatives that go beyond showcasing scientific and technical advancements. These initiatives include a Guinness World Record campaign to promote solar energy use and awareness campaigns about mental health and menstruation

Large-scale events: Techfest culminates in a three-day event on the IIT Bombay campus that attracts people from around the world.
